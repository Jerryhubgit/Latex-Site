Lines

Lines are one of the most easiest diagram to make. All you have to do is to:

  1. Declare that you are using the tikz package using \usepackage{tikz}
  2. To draw, you just specify the start and stop Co-ordinate, in the example (0,0) and (4,0) are the start and stop point respectively

\documentclass{article}
\usepackage{tikz}
\begin{document}
\begin{tikzpicture}
\draw (0,0) -- (4,0);
\end{tikzpicture}
\end{document}
